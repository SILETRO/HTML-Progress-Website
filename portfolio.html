#1
Assistant Sil

This one was the hardest to make. I remember browsing till the 5th google page. Who even needs to search that far? Have you ever?
To get the most accurate weather, I have linked the program to the weather API that fetches data from the most accurate sources.

So basically, Assistant Sil is a program that helps you search your way across the web. Say you want to listen to a song or search for images of the Kardishians before surgery, you just have to type it out and then you'll be directed promptly to the corresponding page. Youtube for the song, and google for the images.
